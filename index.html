About Jessica

Hello! My name is Jessica K. Baumann, and I’m a dual-degree candidate at Northeastern University studying Mathematics and Data Science with an expected graduation in December 2027. I’m passionate about using data to solve complex problems and communicate insights that drive meaningful decisions. I currently maintain a 4.0 GPA and am actively involved in campus organizations including Women in Finance, the Association for Women in Mathematics, and the Northeastern University Dance Company, where I serve as Treasurer. Professionally, I’ve gained experience as an Actuarial Co-op at John Hancock, where I worked on the Global Asset Modeling team using GGY AXIS software for year-end testing and automation projects that improved workflow efficiency. I also worked as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant in Northeastern’s Khoury College of Computer Sciences, helping students understand data science and programming concepts. Starting this December, I will join Wellington Management as a co-op on the Quantitative Trading Research and Analytics team, where I look forward to applying my analytical and mathematical skills to real-world investment strategies. Outside of academics, I enjoy dance choreography, yoga, reading, and travel, which help me balance creativity with technical precision.
